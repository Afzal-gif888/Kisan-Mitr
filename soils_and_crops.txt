# MASTER REGISTRY: INDIAN SOILS & CROPS

## I. MASTER LIST: INDIAN SOIL TYPES
1.  Alluvial Soil: (Indo-Gangetic Plains) - Richest and most widespread.
2.  Black Soil (Regur): (Deccan Lava Plateau) - Ideal for moisture-loving crops like Cotton.
3.  Red & Yellow Soil: (Southern/Eastern Peninsula) - Iron-rich, porous, good for Millets.
4.  Laterite Soil: (Hilly areas with high rainfall) - Acidic, used for Cashews and Coffee.
5.  Arid / Desert Soil: (West Rajasthan) - High salt, low moisture, for drought-resistant crops.
6.  Mountain / Forest Soil: (Himalayas) - Rich in humus and organic matter, for fruits/spices.
7.  Saline & Alkaline Soil: (Coastal/Dry areas) - High salt content, usually infertile.
8.  Peaty & Marshy Soil: (High rainfall/Waterlogged areas) - Heavy, black, and highly organic.

## II. MASTER LIST: INDIAN CROP CATEGORIES

### 1. Food Grains (Cereals)
Rice (Paddy), Wheat, Maize (Corn), Jowar (Sorghum), Bajra (Pearl Millet), Ragi (Finger Millet), Barley.

### 2. Pulses (Legumes)
Gram (Chickpea), Red Gram (Tur/Arhar), Black Gram (Urad), Green Gram (Moong), Lentils (Masur), Peas.

### 3. Oilseeds
Groundnut, Mustard, Rapeseed, Soybean, Sunflower, Sesamum (Til), Linseed, Castor Seed.

### 4. Cash Crops (Industrial)
Sugarcane, Cotton, Jute, Tobacco.

### 5. Plantation Crops
Tea, Coffee, Rubber, Coconut, Areca Nut (Supari), Cocoa.

### 6. Horticulture: Fruits
Mango, Apple, Banana, Citrus (Orange/Lime), Grapes, Pomegranate, Papaya, Guava, Pineapple, Jackfruit, Litchi.

### 7. Horticulture: Vegetables
- Tuber/Root: Potato, Onion, Garlic, Sweet Potato, Carrot, Radish.
- Mainstream: Tomato, Brinjal, Okra (Bhindi), Cauliflower, Cabbage, Green Peas.
- Gourds: Bottle Gourd (Lauki), Bitter Gourd (Karela), Ridge Gourd, Pumpkin, Cucumber.

### 8. Leafy Vegetables
Spinach (Palak), Fenugreek (Methi), Amaranthus (Thotakura), Gongura (Roselle), Coriander, Mint.

### 9. Spices & Condiments
Turmeric, Ginger, Red Chilli, Black Pepper, Cardamom (Green/Black), Cumin (Jeera), Coriander Seeds, Cloves, Cinnamon, Fennel (Saunf), Saffron.

### 10. Medicinal & Aromatic Crops
Ashwagandha, Aloe Vera, Mentha (Mint), Isabgol, Lemon Grass.
